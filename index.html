Just in Case

About Just in Case:

Just in case gives the users the ability to give clear details on what should happen next in case of death or if the user is unable to make any decisions due to an unfortunate accident.

How Just in Case Works:

  1. Type in all your answers. If the question does not apply to you, put NA.
  2. Hit the green submit button for the sections that apply to you.
  3. Scroll to the results sections to see your answer.
  4. Print out info.
  5. Refresh your browser to erase answers.

NOTE: The website does not store your information. Once you you hit the refresh button on your browser your information is gone.
